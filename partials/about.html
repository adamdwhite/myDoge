About myDoge

This app was created for NSS Front-End Capstone project, cohort 21. The purpose is to inform potential dog adopters about the many varied characteristics regarding hundreds of dog breeds. Additionally, potential adopters may answer a questionnaire regarding the specifics of their lifestyle, in an effort to match them with the best breeds to adopt.

The available data may only provide a broad set of guidelines, as it regards characteristics of "pure" breeds. The highly adaptable nature of dogs means that each potential adoptee should be compassionately considered on an individual basis. Furthermore, we should all be aware of the careful considerations regarding ethical treatment and precautions involving the potential adoption of a pure breed.

Each officially-recognized dog breed has been bred through history to represent countless fascinating traits. While crossing of breeds may not be acceptable for show-quality, it is absolutely beneficial to most bloodlines, and will certainly result in wonderful, healthy offspring with infinite combinations of the traits detailed here. Please consider rescuing a dog from a shelter, and older dogs from shelters are most often overlooked, but may be a perfect fit in your given situation.
